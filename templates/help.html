Help / FAQ

ChordBot helps musos analyse songs they like and identify chord progressions that they could add to their own songs.

This shows us the ways harmony can affect a particular song's mood and tone.

How does it work?
The Chordbot software returns a statistical report of common chord progressions in a piece that may be of interest.

It then uses the data its collected to try and creates it's own chord progression that is similar.
